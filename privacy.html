Privacy Policy for SubGuard

Last updated: November 2025

SubGuard (“the App”) is developed by Enes Çınar. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SubGuard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App allows you to save:

  • Subscription details
  • Password vault data
  • Notes and financial entries

This data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded anywhere.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share or sell any personal data to third parties.

4. Security

The App stores your information securely on your device. However, we recommend enabling device-level security such as Face ID or passcode for maximum protection.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: enes@eksado.com
